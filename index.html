Poverty is a massive global problem and we need to fix it. To combat it, I’ve participated in the 40 hour famine, donated to a food drive, and made a website to tell everyone. This website also includes how you can help

Poverty is where someone may not have the necessary things to survive. Such as a bed to sleep in, not enough food, being homeless, and not having a source of income. In extreme cases they may not have any of these things, and can be life threatening.
