  • What You Can Do

    I’ve done a lot with the 40 Hour Famine, and donating to a food drive. Each of these didn’t take that long and they make you nice afterwards. But what can you do? Here's some things that you can do to save the world. Read on to find out.

  • 1. Feed the Hungry is an organisation that started in 1987. It feeds hungry children in Africa. At the moment, they have a fundraiser for meals given to children in the Sudanese area. With only $6 you can feed a hungry children for a month. If you would like to donate here is the link: https://feedthehungry.org.au/give/tahd2024/ The fundraiser is called “Take Away Hunger Day”

  • 2. The 40 Hour Famine is a great way to see what it would be like without food. I did this, and unlike past years, you can do it whenever. It’s as easy as creating an account, choosing you challenge, and doing your challenge! It’s a really great experience. Here is the link: https://40hourfamine.com.au/

  • 3. You could also make meals for the homeless. You may have seen videos of this when watching YouTube or something else. Find something easy to make and that keeps for while, then donate it to people in need.

  • 4. You can also donate something of your belongings to the Salvation Army. They give your belongs to the poor as well as helping you to declutter you house. They do have a reputation of always begging, but they are try to help people. Here is another link you could follow: https://www.salvationarmy.org.au/
